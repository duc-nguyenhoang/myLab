=================
Required Hardware
=================
Make sure you have prepared the following hardware items:
• VisionFive 2
• Micro-SD card (32 GB or more)
• Micro-SD card reader
• PC with Linux/windows
• USB to Serial Converter (FT232RL)
• Power adapter(5V 3A)
• USB Type-C cable
